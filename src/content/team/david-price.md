David Price is a seasoned leader in investment strategy, product innovation, and operations, with over two decades of experience building and scaling companies across consumer, enterprise, and technology sectors. At PWV, David oversees fund operations, LP relations, and portfolio support, driving the firm’s mission to back transformative early-stage companies and deliver long-term value to both founders and limited partners.

David’s entrepreneurial journey spans co-founding multiple startups and delivering strategic guidance to high-growth companies. He is the co-creator of the open-source application framework RedwoodJS, which has fostered a global developer community and enabled startups to build scalable, full-stack applications. As CTO of WR Hambrecht + Co., he led the development of a fintech product powered by machine learning.

With deep expertise in data science, SaaS platforms, and operational excellence, David has led companies from zero to one, scaling their growth while navigating complex challenges. As a biomedical device patent holder, his contributions to advanced medical technologies exemplify his ability to innovate across industries. David has also served as a trusted consultant to Fortune 50 companies, advising on strategy for new business initiatives, strategic investments, and mergers and acquisitions.
